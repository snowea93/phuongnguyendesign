Hi there,

I'm Phuong, a young, passionate digital media designer and concept developer based in Ho Chi Minh city, Vietnam.

My fundamental goal is to contribute in engaging design in every aspect of life, making it a powerful service that gives values to other businesses and industries. I seek to create innovative and unique ideas, bring them to life and witness them make an impact.

I am specialised in Identity design, UX/UI design, Communication design and 2D motion graphics. For me, a good design has to combine aesthetics with function and a good designer has to combine creative thinking with logical thinking. Working with me, I will help you figure out your business values that gain you a strong identity in the market.

There's really no limit in design for me, go big or go home - let's explore all possibilities!
