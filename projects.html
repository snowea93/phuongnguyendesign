Snap

Snap is an iOS app developed to make shopping for food healthier. It allows users to scan barcodes of food products, then analysises the ingredients to calculate the compatibility percentage of the food to the user’s health condition and suggests healthier food if necessary. Detailed meal planning and meal suggestions are also featured to enhance users’ experience.
